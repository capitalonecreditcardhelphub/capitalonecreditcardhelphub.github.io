How to Activate Your Card Online

  1. Open your web browser and visit the official Capital One activation page.
  2. Sign in to your account using your username and password.
  3. Locate your new card under your account dashboard.
  4. Click on the “Activate Card” option.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete activation.
Go to Official Activation Page

Activate by Phone

  1. Call the phone number provided on the sticker attached to your new card.
  2. Enter your card details when prompted.
  3. Verify your identity.
  4. Follow automated instructions to complete activation.

Troubleshooting & Support

If you experience issues during activation:

  • Ensure your internet connection is secure.
  • Double-check your login credentials.
  • Clear your browser cache and try again.
  • Contact customer support using the number on the back of your card.
